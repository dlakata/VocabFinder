How does it work?

Frequency and vocabulary lists are in the form of:
[word] [definition OR frequency]
[word] [definition OR frequency]
...

When given a frequency list, Vocab Finder returns all the words of the text that did not occur in the list, as well as the lowest-frequency words that occurred, for a total of 100 words. {% endblock %}
