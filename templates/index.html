Want to find obscure or difficult words in an article or book?

Find the writing in a .txt file. Literature in the public domain can be obtained easily via The Internet Archive or Project Gutenberg.

To find obscure words, you can upload your own vocabulary or frequency list. Definitions will be found for all legitimate words, if your list doesn't provide them.

If you don't provide a vocabulary list, the text will be compared against approximately 100,000 English words, as sorted by frequency of usage in the British National Corpus.
