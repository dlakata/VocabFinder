Vocab Finder

Want to find obscure or difficult words in an article or book?

Find the writing in a .txt file. Literature in the public domain can be obtained easily via The Internet Archive or Project Gutenberg. If it's easier, feel free to copy and paste the text below.

To find obscure words, you can choose one of the provided vocabulary lists, ranging from lists of English-language word frequency to a list of words chosen by author David Foster Wallace.
Alternatively, you can upload your own vocabulary list. Definitions will be found for all legitimate words.

  • American frequency list (~100,000 words)
  • Movie subtitles freq. list (~80,000 words)
  • British frequency list (~75,000 words)
  • More subtitles freq. list (~60,000 words)
  • Barron's GRE words (800 words)
  • David Foster Wallace's words (360 words)
